About DodlAx

Your Local CNC Drawing & Printing Partner

DodlAx is a hyper-local delivery service that blends automation with creativity. We use CNC pen plotter machines to draw or print your uploaded designs — such as assignments, posters, blueprints or sketches — and deliver them within 3 hours in Haridwar.

Whether you're a student, designer, or working professional, DodlAx helps you skip the hassle of printing or drawing manually. Just upload your design on our app, confirm your order, and sit back — we’ll do the rest.
